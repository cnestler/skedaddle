As city populations swell and refined ride-share (e.g., car and bike) programs gain traction, bus options -- although cheap -- are inefficient. Whether hiking, skiing, venturing to the beach, or going to a music festival, direct mass transit is an obstacle for many. As a web-based, crowdsourced platform for bus rentals, Skedaddle solves this problem by matching supply to demand and allowing riders to design customized routes with ease.
